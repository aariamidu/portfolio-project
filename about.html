About Me

Hello there, I'm Aarifah Amidu, and I'm on this amazing journey from railway engineering to the thrilling world of technology!

Ever since I started teaching myself in September 2022, every day feels like a new adventure. I absolutely love solving problems, and the tech world provides an endless playground for that. It's a journey of constant learning and growth, and I'm excited to keep adding new skills to my toolkit. The tech landscape is always evolving, and being a part of that evolution is both challenging and incredibly rewarding.

So, join me on this genuine and sometimes challenging, but always exciting, ride into the tech realm. Let's learn, grow, and have a good time along the way!
